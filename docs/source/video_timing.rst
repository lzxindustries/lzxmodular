Video Timing
=========================

The current generation of LZX Modular supports 15 video sync formats.  

- NTSC
- PAL
- 480p
- 576p
- 720p50
- 720p5994
- 720p60
- 1080p2398
- 1080p24
- 1080p25
- 1080p2997
- 1080i50
- 1080i5994
- 1080i60

Products released before 2020 supported 2 video sync formats.

- NTSC
- PAL
